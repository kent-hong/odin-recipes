French Fries

Description

Thin homemade French fries are the ultimate comfort food, offering a crispy, golden exterior with a light and tender bite inside. Made from freshly cut potatoes, these fries are sliced into thin strips and then soaked in cold water to remove excess starch, which helps them achieve that perfect crunch when fried. After soaking, they’re thoroughly dried and then fried in hot oil until they reach a beautiful golden-brown color. The result is a batch of fries that are delightfully crisp on the outside, with a soft, fluffy interior that melts in your mouth.

What makes homemade French fries truly special is the ability to customize them to your liking. Whether you prefer them lightly salted or generously seasoned with your favorite spices, these fries can be tailored to suit any taste. Serve them piping hot, straight from the fryer, with a side of ketchup, aioli, or any dipping sauce you love, and you’ve got a snack or side dish that’s as satisfying as it is simple. Whether enjoyed on their own or as an accompaniment to a juicy burger, thin homemade French fries are a timeless treat that never fails to please.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water, or as needed
