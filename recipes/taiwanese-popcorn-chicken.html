Taiwanese Popcorn Chicken

Description

Taiwanese popcorn chicken, or "yán sū jī," is a popular street food that has captured the hearts of food lovers around the world. These bite-sized pieces of chicken are marinated in a flavorful mix of soy sauce, garlic, and five-spice powder, giving them a savory and aromatic taste that’s unmistakably Taiwanese. After marinating, the chicken is coated in sweet potato starch, which gives it a unique, crispy texture when deep-fried. The result is a delightful contrast between the crunchy exterior and the tender, juicy chicken inside, making each bite irresistibly delicious.
